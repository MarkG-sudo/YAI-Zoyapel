About Zoyapel

ZoyaPel Limited produces plant-based mosquito repellents made from natural ingredients, providing safe and long-lasting protection.

Our mission is to offer non-toxic, affordable, and effective mosquito repellents, especially for low- income communities, to ensure safe protection for all.Our vision is to become a leading brand in sustainable mosquito protection, reducing reliance on harmful chemical repellents while improving public health.

Our advanced process, extracts essential oils from citronella, eucalyptus and bushmint to create Zoyapel, transforming a once-overlooked by-product of the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and cosmetics industries.

About the Innovator

Douglas Akolgo, a 30 year-old entrepreneur from Navrongo in Ghana’s Upper East Region, is transforming local plants into sustainable health solution, leveraging on indigenous knowledge.

Growing up in a rural and mosquito prone area, he witnessed firsthand how many people including himself, especially children and pregnant women suffered from malaria and even sometimes losing their lives, coupled with local people using century old system to where plants helped protect households against mosquito bites, but was done in an ineffective way due to the continued list of the indigenous knowledge.

Determined to create a solution for himself and other members of his community, he developed an innovative method to extract essential oils from citronella, eucalyptus and bushmint and with the mixture of other ingredients, specially formulate an affordable and effective mosquito repellent.
