About Zoyapel

ZoyaPel Limited creates plant-based mosquito repellents from natural ingredients for safe, long-lasting protection.

We aim to provide non-toxic, affordable repellents, especially for low-income communities, while promoting sustainable mosquito protection.

Using advanced extraction, we derive essential oils from citronella, eucalyptus, and bushmint, transforming once-overlooked by-products into ZoyaPel.
